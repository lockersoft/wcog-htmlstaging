Smart Trips Coordinator Toolkit

Connect your employees to more affordable, active, and sustainable transportation options like biking, riding the bus, walking, or sharing a ride using these materials.

Download files and copy content to use in company emails, new employee packets, bulletin boards, digital monitors and more. Access helpful STC instruction sheets. View other employer partner materials for inspiration. Motivate your employees to participate.

Do you have questions, ideas, support needs, or want to request a customized flyer, poster, or sign? Email blanche@wcog.org for help!

Here are your tools! Click on the plus buttons below to view examples and download materials.

Customized Worksite Flyer

We can customize a Smart Trips flyer outlining your specific program benefits. This is an excellent resource for new employees. Available in digital and printed brochure formats.

Smart Way to Get to Work

This is a generic flyer aimed at new employees outlining resources and the benefits of participating in Smart Trips and biking, walking, taking the bus or sharing a ride to work

Smart Trips Program

Explains the rewards available to Smart Trip makers, such as prize drawings and the Smart Trips Discount. Also available in printed brochure format.

Smart Trips Map of Bellingham

Shows trails, bicycle routes, bus routes and bus stops as well as many businesses and destinations that can be accessed on foot, by bicycle and by bus. Also available in print. Ask about laminated copies for display purposes.

Smart Trips Map of Whatcom County

Shows trails, bicycle routes, bus routes and bus stops in Whatcom County as well as inset maps of Blaine, Everson, Ferndale, Lynden, Nooksack, Point Roberts, and Sumas. Also available in print. Ask about laminated copies for display purposes.

Confident City Cycling

Provides tips for sharing the road safely and confidently with motorists. Also available in printed brochure format

Emergency Ride Home

Explains how and when to use the free emergency taxi service operated by WTA for Smart Trip makers and bus riders. Also available in printed rack card format.

Benefits of Your Employees Making Smart Trips

Gives your employees a list of reasons to participate in Smart Trips

Customized Winner Poster

If one of your employees wins a Smart Trips prize drawing, we can customize a poster highlighting your employee with a photo and quote about the type of Smart Trips they make. Available in digital and printed brochure formats.

Customized Smart Trips Coordinator Sign

Let us customize a sign listing your name and contact information for use in breakrooms, bulletin boards, or your office door. Available in digital format for you to print, or we can print and laminate for you.

Communitywide Stats Poster

Each year Smart Trips tallies all statistic captured by everyone’s Smart Trips Diary entries. Share these impressive numbers with employees. Show them what they’re contributing to. Customized employer posters showing only your employee’s stats are available in print and digital format.
